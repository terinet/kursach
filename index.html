OUR MISSION

At Enhancing Digital Inclusion, we are committed to providing user-centered design solutions for older
adults in Irpin. Our mission is to ensure that every older
adult has the opportunity to embrace technology and
benefit from the digital world. By fostering digital
inclusion, we aim to create a more connected,
empowered, and engaged community of older adults.

Learn more about the services we offer

Enhancing Digital Inclusion: User-Centered Design for Older Adults provides a wide range of services for the entire Irpin region.

Digital Literacy Workshops

Engage in interactive workshops to improve digital skills and confidence.

Tech Support Services

Receive personalized assistance and troubleshooting for tech issues.

Digital Wellness Seminars

Participate in informative seminars to promote digital well-being.
